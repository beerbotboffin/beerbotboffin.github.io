Arno Avengers Thing

Covid Isolation Gift, 4 April, 2020

A simple gift for a colleague at work. I, like a lot of other people (note: a lot, not alot. Alot is not a word) and found that I had a little spare time on my hands and an itch to make something. Arno is an big Marvel fan, so I designed this for him as a gift in Openscad. As I don't own a multicolour printer, I designed it as a multi-part piece printed in difference colours and assembled post printing.
