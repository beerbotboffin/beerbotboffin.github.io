Cat Feeder

A project to be able to feed my cat Max when I'm away or busy, 17 Jun, 2021

I have a little mate who's good for company, but like most cats gets unhappy if he's not fed on time, every single time.

What's a pain is that as I live alone I can't really go anywhere overnight as I have a ravenous little mouth to feed. Or if I'm in the office I come to home cries of 'Where have you been? I'm STARVING TO DEATH HERE!!!'.

Looking to solve both problems, I've been working on a feeder system where I can pre-load small cans of cat food into a machine and have it dispense said wet food either on a scheduled basis or controlled remotely. The system involves a gravity fed can feeder which dispenses cans to a robot arm which is then used to place the can in the can rotator, then move the can to the can opener, then dispense the contents of the can into the cat bowl and finally place the can in a waste bin. Why so complex? Well, I'm trying to find a second cat to keep Max happy and so will need to dispense 2 cans at the same time. This system will allow for custom menus for either cat. Not that cats are fussy, not at all

So far, I have added a bot channel to Telegram and a bot controller in NodeRed. This allows me to a). send commands to the cat feeder and b). get a video back showing that the process has indeed worked and that said cat(s) is happy and fed. I can also just get a video of the general situation to check on the general state of things, from anywhere in the world!
