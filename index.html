3D Printed Monitor Arms

Monitor arms are expensive, so let's design / make our own 1 Jul, 2021

Finally got the motivation to get the multi-monitor setup, set up. I have a number of LG E2340V monitors which whilst are good, are a bit retarded in that they lack VESA mounts. No worries, I have a 3D printer (OK, several 3D printers).

My desk is a corner desk and I'd like to go 3 monitors wide and 2 high for a total of 6 monitors. I'm currently at 4 monitors so it's 3 wide with an extra in the middle

I used OpenScad for the design and printed in eSun PLA+. You may note the 2 shades of white which is because I ordered white instead of cold white like I did last time. Oh well. You wouldn't have known if I didn't say anything, would you?

Automated / Remote Controlled Cat Feeder

A project to be able to feed my cat Max when I'm away or busy, 17 Jun, 2021

I have a little mate who's good for company, but like most cats gets unhappy if he's not fed on time, every single time.

What's a pain is that as I live alone I can't really go anywhere overnight as I have a ravenous little mouth to feed. Or if I'm in the office I come to home cries of 'Where have you been? I'm STARVING TO DEATH HERE!!!'.

Looking to solve both problems, I've been working on a feeder system where I can pre-load small cans of cat food into a machine and have it dispense said wet food either on a scheduled basis or controlled remotely.

BMW Garage Wheels

3D Printed Omni-wheels for the BMW, 4 Jul 2020

I own a BMW R1200RT which I love. It's a great bike, gobbling up the km's like it's nothing at all. The trouble is manoeuvring and storing it in the garage.

So, I've decided to try 3D printing mecanum (omni) wheels which will mount to the centre stand and allow the old girl to be manoeuvred around laterally 'at the hips' in the garage (smooth floor). You see the issue isn't so much the space (though it's not a small bike by anyone's standards) - it's how unwieldy it is at slow speed.
And the panniers don't help, either.
